learning new things

My hobby is the pursuit of knowledge and personal growth through continuous learning. I thrive on exploring new subjects, acquiring new skills, and gaining insights into various aspects of life. Whether it's diving into books, online courses, or hands-on experiences, I find joy in the journey of discovery. This hobby not only broadens my horizons but also keeps me engaged and motivated to embrace new challenges and opportunities that come my way.

play kabaddi

Playing Kabaddi is an exhilarating and physically demanding sport that I enjoy. As a team sport, it fosters camaraderie and teamwork. When I step onto the Kabaddi mat, I become the raider, using agility and strategy to tag opponents and return safely to my side. It's a thrilling experience, both physically and mentally, as I face off against defenders trying to stop me. Kabaddi not only keeps me fit but also sharpens my decision-making skills, making it my favorite hobby.

reading nobal

My hobby is immersing myself in the rich and diverse world of literature by Hindi Nobel Laureates. Exploring the works of Indian authors who have received the Nobel Prize in Literature is a source of immense joy and enlightenment. Their writings reflect the beauty of the Hindi language, the depth of Indian culture, and the universal themes that resonate with readers worldwide. With each book I read, I not only appreciate the literary excellence but also gain insights into the human experience, making this hobby a meaningful and rewarding journey of discovery.

listing songs

My hobby is curating and listing songs from various genres and eras. I find joy in creating playlists for different moods, occasions, or themes. Whether it's assembling a collection of feel-good tunes for a road trip, compiling a soothing playlist for relaxation, or organizing a nostalgic list of classics, I immerse myself in the world of music. This hobby allows me to explore the vast landscape of melodies, lyrics, and rhythms, and share the gift of music with friends and family. It's a hobby that not only entertains but also brings people together through the universal language of songs.
